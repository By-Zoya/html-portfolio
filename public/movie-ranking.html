List of best horror movies according to Zoya

My top 3 movies

  1. Hereditary

    2. A slow-burn psychological horror about a grieving family and something much darker lurking underneath.

    Review:

    This one isn’t just scary—it’s disturbing. It builds tension quietly and then hits hard. Not for casual watching; it stays in your head after.

  2. The Conjuring

    3. Based on paranormal investigators dealing with a haunted family home.

    Review:

    Classic haunted house done really well. Strong atmosphere, solid jump scares, and a story that actually pulls you in instead of relying only on cheap thrills.

  3. Coraline

    4. An animated story about a girl who finds a “better” version of her world… that turns sinister.

    Review:

    Looks like a kids’ movie, but it’s secretly creepy. The “Other Mother” concept is unsettling in a very clever way. Perfect if you want eerie without extreme horror.
